Classic Homemade Pizza

Description

Nothing beats a warm, cheesy homemade pizza! This recipe guides you through making a classic pizza with your favorite toppings, whether you're starting with store-bought dough or making your own. It's versatile, fun to customize, and sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1.5 - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for drizzling)
  • Optional: Dried oregano or fresh basil for garnish

Preparation:

  1. Step 1: Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If you have a pizza stone or steel, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Step 2: Prepare Dough: On a lightly floured surface, roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape (round or rectangular). Carefully transfer it to a baking sheet, pizza peel, or directly onto your preheated pizza stone/steel.
  3. Step 3: Add Sauce and Cheese: Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Step 4: Add Toppings: Arrange your chosen toppings evenly over the cheese. Drizzle a little olive oil over the edges of the crust for a golden finish.
  5. Step 5: Bake: Bake for 12-18 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned. Baking time may vary depending on your oven and chosen crust thickness.
  6. Step 6: Serve: Carefully remove the pizza from the oven. Let it cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
