Homemade Doner Kebab Recipe

Description

Craving a classic doner kebab but want to make it at home? This recipe simplifies the process, allowing you to create tender, flavorful meat, crisp vegetables, and a creamy sauce, all wrapped in warm pita bread. It's a perfect street food favorite, recreated in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Meat:

  • 1 pound ground lamb or beef (or a mix)
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • ½ cup plain yogurt (Greek yogurt recommended)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill or mint (optional)
  • Salt to taste

For Serving:

  • 4-6 pita breads or flatbreads
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced red onion
  • Cucumber, thinly sliced

Preparation:

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Meat: In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, grated onion (squeeze out excess liquid), minced garlic, cumin, paprika, oregano, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly until all ingredients are well combined and the mixture is slightly sticky.
  2. Step 2: Shape and Cook the Meat: Form the meat mixture into a compact loaf shape (like a small meatloaf or sausage). You can pan-fry this in a little oil until browned on all sides and cooked through, or bake it at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes. Once cooked, let it rest for 5-10 minutes, then slice it very thinly.
  3. Step 3: Make the Sauce: While the meat cooks, combine yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, and fresh herbs (if using) in a small bowl. Season with salt to taste. Mix well and set aside.
  4. Step 4: Prepare Accompaniments: Warm the pita breads in a dry pan, oven, or microwave until soft and pliable. Prepare all your desired vegetable toppings.
  5. Step 5: Assemble the Doner: Open the warm pita bread to create a pocket or lay it flat. Fill with a generous amount of the thinly sliced doner meat. Add shredded lettuce, sliced tomatoes, red onion, and cucumber. Drizzle generously with the prepared yogurt sauce.
  6. Step 6: Serve: Fold or roll your homemade doner kebab and enjoy immediately!
